What is Environmental change?

A change or disturbance of the environment is called Environmntal change

What causes Environmental change ?

It is often caused by human influences and natural ecological processes. Environmental changes include various factors, such as natural disasters, human interferences, or animal interaction. Environmental change encompasses not only physical changes, but also factors like an infestation of invasive species.

Types of Environmetal change:

  • Global Warming and Climate change
  • Water Pollution and Ocean Acidification
  • Loss of Bio-diversity
